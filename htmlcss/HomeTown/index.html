Dinner Cruise

Enjoy a delightful dinner cruise on the Saigon River, taking in the stunning views of the city skyline and savoring delicious Vietnamese cuisine.

The Myst Dong Khoi

Experience Saigon’s charm at The Myst Dong Khoi, where unique design, local artifacts, and warm hospitality immerse you in the city's vibrant culture.

Admire the iconic twin bell towers, stunning stained-glass windows, and tranquil atmosphere of this historic cathedral—one of Ho Chi Minh City's most prominent landmarks.
